Creme de Menthe

Description

A delicious pie made of marshmallow, oreo crumbs, and flavored with a splash of liqueur.

Ingredients

  • 1 3/4 cup of mini marshmallows
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • 2/3 cup half-and-half
  • 1 1/4 cup oreo crumbs
  • 3 tbsp butter, melted
  • 2 tsp creme de menthe liqueur
  • 2 tsp creme de cacao liqueur

Steps

  1. Place the marshmallows and half-and-half in a medium/large saucepan and melt over low heat, stirring occasionally
  2. As it melts, combine melted butter with oreo crumbs, mixing thoroughly
  3. Place oreo crumbs in a pie dish and place in the freezer to cool
  4. Whip the heavy cream
  5. Once the marshmallows have melted, place them in a bowl with ice water to cool the mixture
  6. Gently fold the whipped heavy creme into the marshmallow mixture
  7. Add the liqueurs to the mixture and gently stir to combine
  8. Pour the mixture into the oreo crumb crust, and place in a refrigerator to allow it to set
