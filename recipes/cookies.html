Cookies

Description

You know the drill, they're chocolate chip cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 cup softened butter
  • 2/3 cup white sugar
  • 1/3 cup brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups flour
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 package chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Combine butter and sugar until well mixed
  2. Add eggs and vanilla and mix in
  3. In a separate bowl, mix together flour and baking powder
  4. slowly mix flour into the other stuff
  5. once all mixed, add in the chocolate chips (maybe eat some for yourself though they're delightful
